Soft Baby Girl & Boy Sandals

Designed for comfort, style, and safety, these baby sandals are perfect for every stage of your little one's journey.

With an anti-slip rubber sole, every step is secure and supported, helping your child walk confidently while staying comfortable all day long. The adjustable hook and loop closure makes it easy for parents to put them on and take them off without hassle.
