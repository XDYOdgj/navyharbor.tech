Leveret Long Sleeve Turtleneck Bodysuit

A soft, stylish, and durable addition to your baby's wardrobe made from 100% cotton.

Soft & Gentle Fabric

The Leveret bodysuit is crafted from ultra-soft 100% cotton interlock fabric, ensuring comfort for your baby's delicate skin. With a 190 GSM weight, it's breathable and gentle, making it perfect for everyday wear.

Extra Warmth & Protection

The turtleneck design covers your baby's neck, providing an extra layer of warmth and comfort during cooler weather or as a base layer. It helps keep your little one cozy while staying stylish and secure.

Stain-Resistant & Durable

This bodysuit is designed to resist stains, making cleaning easier than ever. Its durable construction ensures it maintains its shape, color, and softness after multiple washes, offering lasting value.

Easy Care & Versatile Fit

Easily machine washable and recommended to be turned inside out for optimal care, this bodysuit is a convenience for busy parents. Available in sizes 6 Months to 2 Toddler, it's designed to fit snugly without restricting movement.

Whether you're preparing for a baby shower, a day at the park, or just a cozy night at home, the Leveret long sleeve turtleneck bodysuit is a versatile and reliable choice for your little one's wardrobe.
