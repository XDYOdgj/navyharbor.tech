The Smartwool Women's Classic Thermal Merino Baselayer Crew

Experience ultimate comfort and performance with this premium thermal baselayer crafted for active lifestyles. Designed with 100% Merino Wool, it offers natural warmth and softness that adapt to your body temperature, keeping you cozy in the cold and fresh when it's warmer.

Our imported fabric ensures durability and a smooth, irritation-free feel against your skin. The regular fit provides freedom of movement, making it ideal for layering under jackets, sweaters, or other outdoor gear. The interlock knit enhances breathability and keeps you dry during intense activity, while its odor-resistant technology ensures all-day freshness, no matter how long you wear it.

Why Choose This Baselayer?

  • 100% Merino Wool: Offers natural warmth, softness, and moisture-wicking properties for all-day comfort.
  • Imported Quality Fabric: Built to last with premium durability and a smooth touch against your skin.
  • Regular Fit: Provides unrestricted movement without adding bulk, ideal for outdoor and casual wear.
  • Interlock Knit: Delivers breathability and reduces friction, ensuring a comfortable fit even during physical activity.
  • Odor-Resistant Technology: Keeps you feeling fresh with long-lasting hygiene, perfect for extended wear.

Whether you're exploring the outdoors, running errands, or staying active in chilly weather, this thermal baselayer is your go-to choice for comfort and style. Its versatile design makes it suitable for all seasons and a must-have in any woman's wardrobe.
