Trekkie Baby Onesie with Hat and Blanket Set

Introduce your little Starfleet explorer to the world with this charming and functional baby outfit set. Featuring a classic Starfleet insignia in bold black design, it's a must-have for any Trekkie family. Perfect for space-themed sleepovers or cozy bedtime routines, this set includes a onesie, hat, and blanket, all crafted with care and comfort in mind.

Key Features

  • Lap shoulder neckline for easy dressing
  • Bottom snap closure for effortless changing
  • One-size-fits-all hat for newborns up to 6 months
  • 35x30 inch blanket, ideal for cozy comfort
  • 100% cotton interlock knit for softness and durability

Why Choose This Set?

Every detail of this Trekkie baby set is designed with your little one's comfort and style in mind. The onesie is made from high-quality 100% cotton with CPSIA certified heat transfer vinyl, ensuring safety and durability for sensitive skin. The hat, with its soft outer and inner brim, fits securely and stays stylish as your baby grows. The blanket offers the perfect warmth and coziness for bedtime or nap time, creating a comforting and nostalgic atmosphere for your little Starfleet fan.

Machine washable and handmade, this set is not only functional but also a unique and personal gift. Whether it's for a new baby or a special occasion, this Trekkie baby outfit is the ideal way to celebrate your child's love for space and adventure.
